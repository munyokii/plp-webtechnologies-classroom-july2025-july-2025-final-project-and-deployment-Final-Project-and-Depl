Frequently Asked Questions

What types of produce do you grow?
We grow a variety of seasonal vegetables including tomatoes, lettuce, carrots, peppers, and herbs. We also have fruit trees producing apples, pears, and seasonal berries.
Are your products organic?
Yes! All our produce is certified organic. We use natural farming methods without synthetic pesticides or fertilizers, ensuring the highest quality for our customers.
Do you offer farm tours?
Absolutely! We offer guided tours every Saturday at 10 AM and 2 PM. Tours include a walk through our fields, greenhouse visit, and fresh produce tasting.
What are your market hours?
Our farm stand is open Tuesday through Sunday, 8 AM to 6 PM. We're closed on Mondays for restocking and maintenance.
